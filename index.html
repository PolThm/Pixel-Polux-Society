The story

It all started a long while ago. At the time, the internet barely existed. One of the few existing computers was the minitel. Following the mishandling of a mad developer, a program got carried away and created the first Polux (The Precursor).

This personage quickly felt alone in this device, therefore he decided to repeat the bug to create 99 other characters to found the Pixel Polux Society. All of these people dreamed of greatness and they felt very cramped inside this extremely slow minitel.

That 's why a small group of 10 particularly special Polux joined forces and found a spatio-temporal rift to escape this dull era to join the Metaverse, where they finally discovered color... Fortunately for us, they still keep their pixels that remind us of these beautiful vintage days!
